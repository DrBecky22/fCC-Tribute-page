Nicolas Cage

Compellingly Eccentric, an actor for our strange times

A Timeline of Nick Cage's Career

  • 1964 - born in Long Beach, California
  • 1981 - acting debut in "The Best of Times" never aired
  • 1983 - lead male role in Valley Girl
  • 1987 - Breakthrough roles in Peggy Sue got Married and Raising Arizona bringing him to national stardom
  • 1990's - Career Slump
  • 1995 - Career comeback with Leaving Las Vegas
  • 2000-present - Long string of weirdly compelling eccentric characters

"I'm at the point now where I know I'm doing something right when a movie gets mixed reviews, because then I'm not in the box. I don't want to make it too easy for people and I don't want to make it too easy for myself. I want to try something unusual. I feel good about the bad reviews because I feel like I've affected them on some level. They may not know what I was trying to do but they felt something."

-- Nicholas Cage on his work
